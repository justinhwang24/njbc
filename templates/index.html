What We Do

Located in Bergen County, NJ, we organize regular tournaments across the region. With hundreds of participants, our events attract many players with wide-ranging skill levels. All are welcome to join!

Who We Are

NJBC Volleyball is a non-profit organization founded by volleyball lovers in Bergen County, NJ, with the goal of getting people together to play the sport we all enjoy.

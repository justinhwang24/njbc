What We Do

Located in Bergen County, NJ, we organize regular tournaments and meetups across the region. Our events attract hundreds of players with wide-ranging skill levels who share a common interest in volleyball. All are welcome to join!

Tournaments

We host regular tournaments with cash prizes across Bergen County. Results will be counted towards the leaderboard.

Meetups

We host meetups across Bergen County, which are more are mainly for laid-back, casual practice.

Community

We have an active community of hundreds of members. Feel free to contact us or join our Discord!

Meet The Team

We are a non-profit organization consisting of individuals who all share a common interest in volleyball. Founded in spring of 2022, our goal is to grow the sport by organizing events within our community to bring volleyball lovers together.

Founder Greetings

My fellow co-founders and I always wanted to create and maintain a volleyball community. To make this vision a reality, we first started with a simple Discord and now run tournaments. In the short time that our organization has been created, our community has grown to nearly 220 individuals.

Our hope is that every single person (beginner, intermediate, or expert) can find our organization’s events a welcoming and hospitable community. We thank every member and team members’ cooperation and commitment to this cause.

We hope that we can meet your expectations and hope that you will be able to join us. Once again, thank you for your continued support.

Sincerely,
Founder Issac Cho
